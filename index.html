How it works

Set up in minutes.
Works on any website.

1

Sign up and get your personal contact form link

You receive a unique StayOffMySite URL. This becomes your website's contact form.

2

Embed it on your site in one line

Replace your existing contact form with your StayOffMySite link. Paste it as a button, a link, or an iframe. No developer needed.

3

Add people to your block, flag, or quarantine list

Block by email to stop a known address dead. Flag an email to let it through but into a separate queue. Quarantine by name to catch anyone using that name regardless of the email address they use.

4

Every blocked attempt is logged automatically

StayOffMySite records every blocked or quarantined submission with a timestamp and the content they tried to send. That record is yours, always accessible from your dashboard.

5

Legitimate messages reach you as normal

Everyone not on your list contacts you exactly as before. You get their message. You stay in control.
